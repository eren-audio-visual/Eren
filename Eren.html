About

Eren is a rising artist on a path to marry the musical worlds of electronic music and soundtracks. Originally from California, Eren has made Georgia his home forging a musical acumen around electronic and house music. Legendary composers like Hans Zimmer raised Eren—giving him his first taste of music. Eren wants to cultivate auditory safe spaces on GSU’s campuses and beyond—spaces where audiences can calmly consume his ethereal sounds. With his visuals from TouchDesigner, Eren creates evocative audio-visual experiences and he’s here to do it again.Beyond his duties as a student, Eren enjoys playing with synthesizers and watching films.
